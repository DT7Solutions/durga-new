Shipping & Returns Policy

At Durga Ghee, we strive to ensure your orders are delivered promptly and safely. Please read the following details regarding our shipping and returns process:

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 2 to 3 business days after confirmation. You will be notified once your order is packed and ready to ship.

2. Shipping Time

Standard shipping within India takes approximately 5 to 7 business days. Please note that delays may occur during peak seasons or due to unforeseen courier issues.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout. We offer free shipping on all orders.

4. Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email and/or SMS to monitor your shipment in real-time.

5. Delivery Partners

We partner with trusted courier services such as DTDC to ensure reliable and timely deliveries.
